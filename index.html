Austin Power's Hangman
Yeah Baby!

    Instructions

  1. Press the space key to start
  2. You can play up to 10 rounds of hangman
  3. Each word or short phase is based on an Austin Power's movie
  4. Press a letter key to select a letter
  5. Each time you miss a letter, a part of Austin Power's gold nemesis will appear
  6. If you miss more than 6 letters, you lose the round.
  7. Press the escape key to end the game
